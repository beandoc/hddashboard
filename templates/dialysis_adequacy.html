info eKt/V resolution hierarchy (RA HD Guideline 2019):
  1. Direct — equilibrated Kt/V entered in monthly record
  2. Tattersall — eKt/V = (spKt/V × t) / (t + 35) for AVF/AVG, or (t + 22) for catheter
  3. Daugirdas — spKt/V from pre/post urea + UF volume, then Tattersall applied
  4. URR log-ratio — ln(100/(100−URR)); valid for 4-hour fistula sessions at UF/TW ≈ 0.02
Combined eKt/V (method A, RA HD 2019): eKt/VKidney = Krcr × 5500 / (580 × TW); Combined = eKt/VDialysis + eKt/VKidney. Requires Krcr (ml/min) in monthly record. "✓ via combined" marks patients reaching target only when residual function is included.
EKRc (method B, Casino & Lopez): EKRc = 1 + (10 × eKt/V) + Kru; target ≥ 13 ml/min for thrice-weekly. In the absence of residual function an eKt/V of 1.2 equates exactly to EKRc 13 ml/min.

water_drop HDF Filtration (Convective) Volume — Appendix C, RA HD Guideline 2019: Average filtration volume = actual UF volume per HDF session. Convective clearance for small solutes (urea) is negligible relative to diffusion. Convection is clinically significant for middle molecules (e.g. β₂-microglobulin). Pre-dilution HDF filtration volumes are adjusted downward by the dilutional effect of upstream replacement fluid.
