Each patient's current access episode is scored across 11 clinical risk factors (thrombosis history, Qa decline, recirculation, steal grade, cannulation failure rate, KDOQI 1-2-3 rule breaches, and more). Score is normalized to a 0–100% risk index. Patients are ranked by risk — top at-risk patients surface here for proactive intervention.
