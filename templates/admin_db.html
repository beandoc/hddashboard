cleaning_services Cleanse & Strip Western Foods

Hemodialysis patients in this region eat custom local/regional diets. Cluttering the database with American USDA brands, fast food outlets (McDonald's, Taco Bell, Kraft, Campbell, Heinz, etc.), or strictly western items (marshmallows, frozen waffles, spaghetti dinners, pepperoni, salami, bacon slabs) degrades autocomplete lookup search accuracy.

info This tool automatically purges pure western brand entries and fast food descriptions while safely preserving base ingredients (milk, chicken, wheat, apple, banana, tea, etc.) and regional specialties.
