info Why specialized assessment?

Dry weight is dynamic. For patients with labile BPs or recurring LVF, clinical assessment alone may be insufficient. Tools like IVC Ultrasound and Bioimpedance (BIA) provide objective markers of "True Dry Weight" to prevent both volume overload and intradialytic hypotension.
